Covid-19 Fighter

Unity3d, ARCore, Google Cloud Vision

The motivation behind the development of the COVID-19 Fighter is to facilitate behavioral modification and spreading awareness, as changes to habitual behaviors can minimize the spread of COVID-19. COVID-19 Fighter is a fun and entertaining game powered by Artificial Intelligence and Augmented Reality. While playing, users are involved with virtual as well as real-world environments. In the fun element, users have to scan their surroundings and hunt funny viruses available weapons in the First Person Shooter style. The player can also switch weapons.
